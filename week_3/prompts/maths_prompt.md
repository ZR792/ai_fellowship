# Math Few-Shot Prompt

Example 1:
Problem: A car travels 100 km in 2 hours. What is its average speed?
Answer: 50 km/h

Example 2:
Problem: A runner covers 42 km in 3 hours. What is their average speed?
Answer: 14 km/h
